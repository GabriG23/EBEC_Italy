EBEC Italy Torino 2022

DESIGN THE FUTURE. TODAY.

The National Round of EBEC Italy is one of the most challenging rounds of the EBEC Pyramid, gathering the best 40 students who have won their Local Round in Turin, Milan, Naples, Rome Sapienza and Rome Tor Vergata.

The finalists are highly talented and skilled students immersed in a dynamic, multicultural and innovative environment where students, companies and universities share and gain knowledge about the world of tech.
