EBEC Italy Messina 2021

DESIGN THE FUTURE. TODAY.

The National Round of EBEC Italy is one of the most challenging rounds of the EBEC Pyramid, gathering the best 48 students who have won their Local Round in Turin, Milan, Naples, Messina, Roma Sapienza and Rome Tor Vergata.

The finalists are highly talented and skilled students immersed in a dynamic, multicultural and innovative environment where students, companies and universities share and gain knowledge about the world of tech.
