1. HOW CAN A STUDENT PARTICIPATE IN EBEC ITALY?

To participate in EBEC Italy you need to win the Local Round of EBEC held in your university. The Italian universities that organize an EBEC Local Round are: Politecnico di Torino, Politecnico di Milano, Università degli Studi di Napoli Federico II, Università degli Studi di Roma Tor Vergata, Università degli Studi di Roma Sapienza.

2. WHEN WILL THE FINAL ROUND OF EBEC BE HELD?

The Final Round of EBEC will be held from the 22 to the 30 of July.


3. WHERE WILL THE FINAL ROUND OF EBEC BE HELD?

The Final Round of EBEC will be held in Zagreb, Croatia.
